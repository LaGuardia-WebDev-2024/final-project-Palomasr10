Cooking Origins

Mauricio (age 6)

Liza Backal

Mauricio learned to cook by watching his grandmother, Liza, who cooked for his family every weekend. She herself learned to cook from her mother, and being the eldest daughter she helped cook for her family constantly. She could make almost any dish. It wasn't until Mauricio was older and away from home that he began using the skills he had absorbed from watching his grandmother so often, and that is where he began his cooking journey and truly began to love to cook and hone his craft.
